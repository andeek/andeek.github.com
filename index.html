I am a postdoc at Duke University working with Beka Steorts in the Department of Statistical Science.

I completed my Ph.D. in Statistics at Iowa State University working with Dan Nordman and Steve Vardeman in the Summer of 2017. I also have an M.S. in Statistics from Iowa State University with Heike Hofmann and Dan Nordman and an M.A. in Mathematics at The University of Texas at Austin under the direction of John Luecke and Martha Smith. I received my B.S. in Mathematics with a certificate in Computing from The University of Texas at Austin.

I'm interested in the intersection of Statistics and Computing, with a penchant for statistical graphics and reproducibility. I like struggling with JavaScript and learning new languages, R being my first love.

When I can, I enjoy racing cyclocross in the fall and cooking/eating delicious food year-round.
